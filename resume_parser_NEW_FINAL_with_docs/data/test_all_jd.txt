
# Job Description: Senior Data Engineering Architect

**Location:** Hybrid (Bangalore/Hyderabad)

**Company:** TechInnovate Solutions

## About the Role

We are seeking an experienced **Senior Data Engineering Architect** to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in designing and implementing scalable data solutions using cloud technologies, with a focus on AWS services. This role will be responsible for architecting, developing, and optimizing our data infrastructure to support our AI and machine learning initiatives.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and develop robust, scalable data pipelines and ETL processes using PySpark, AWS EMR, and other Big Data technologies
- Lead the migration of existing data platforms from on-premises to cloud environments
- Implement data storage solutions using various databases including Snowflake, PostgreSQL, and NoSQL databases
- Architect and develop microservices for data processing using Java/Spring Boot
- Establish best practices for data quality, security, and governance
- Collaborate with data scientists and ML engineers to build data platforms that support AI/ML workloads
- Mentor junior engineers and provide technical leadership to the team

## Required Skills & Experience

- 8+ years of experience in data engineering or similar roles
- Strong expertise in AWS cloud services (EMR, Lambda, S3, RDS)
- Proficiency in PySpark, SQL, and Python for large-scale data processing
- Experience with Snowflake and other modern data warehouse solutions
- Knowledge of microservices architecture and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Familiarity with real-time data processing technologies (Kafka)
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related field

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with AI/ML technologies including LangChain, OpenAI, and vector databases (ChromaDB)
- Certifications such as AWS Solutions Architect or SnowPro Core
- Experience with data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau)
- Knowledge of DevOps practices and CI/CD pipelines
- Experience in financial services, healthcare, or e-commerce industries
- Track record of improving system performance and reducing operational costs

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary package with performance bonuses
- Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies
- Professional development and certification support
- Flexible work arrangements with hybrid options
- Health and wellness benefits
- Collaborative and innovative team environment

This is a unique opportunity to make a significant impact in a fast-growing technology company that values innovation and technical excellence. If you have a passion for designing scalable data solutions and want to work with a talented team pushing the boundaries of what's possible with data, we want to hear from you!
