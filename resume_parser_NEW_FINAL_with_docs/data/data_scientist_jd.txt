# Data Scientist

## About the Role
We are seeking a skilled Data Scientist to join our analytics team. In this role, you will extract insights from complex datasets, develop statistical models, and help drive data-informed decision making across the organization.

## Required Experience
- 3+ years of experience in data science or related analytical role
- Proven track record of applying statistical methods to solve business problems
- Experience with data manipulation, analysis, and visualization tools
- Background in developing machine learning models for prediction and classification
- History of communicating technical findings to non-technical stakeholders

## Required Skills
- Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn)
- SQL
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)
- Statistical Analysis
- Machine Learning
- A/B Testing
- Hypothesis Testing
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
- Feature Engineering

## Nice-to-Have Skills
- R programming language
- Experience with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop)
- Knowledge of deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Natural Language Processing
- Time Series Analysis
- Bayesian Statistics
- Experiment Design
- Dashboard creation (Tableau, Power BI)

## Responsibilities
- Analyze large, complex datasets to extract actionable insights
- Build predictive models and machine learning algorithms
- Design and evaluate A/B tests to measure the impact of product changes
- Create visualizations and dashboards to communicate findings
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and business teams
- Develop reproducible analysis pipelines
- Stay current with advancements in data science techniques and tools

## Qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication abilities, both written and verbal
- Curiosity-driven approach to exploring data
- Ability to translate business questions into analytical frameworks

## Seniority Level
Mid-level

## Job Type
Full-time, On-site

## Industry
Technology, E-commerce

We provide a collaborative work environment, competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional development. Join us to help build data-driven solutions that impact millions of users.
